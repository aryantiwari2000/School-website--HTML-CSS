Admission Procedure

  • 1. The school admits boys and girls from the age of4 yrs onwards.
  • 2. Except for Pre-Primary classes, admission to a class is based on child's performance in the interview.
  • 3. Guardian of the candidates selected for admission are required to present a photocopy of the report card of the previous class and Transfer certificate from the school last attendant at the time of admission.

    • Admission Session

    School's academic session is from April to March.

    School Diary

    School diary is a link between the school and parents. Parents are requested to sign the diary of theri ward regularly to get updated about remarks and information concerning the child.

    Attendance

  • 1. Parents are requested to ensure that their wards attend the school. regularly in case of any absence, parents should submit an application to the Principal.
  • 2. Long leaves will not be allowed to the students during teaching days except on medical grounds.
  • 3. 80% attendance is compulsory to appear in the terminal examination.

    • Fee

  • 1.Fees must be paid positively between 1ST to 10th of every month at the school counter, Failing this the same will be accepted with a late fee Rs. 50/-
  • 2.If you fail to deposit the fee till the end of the month. The name of your ward will be automatically struck off from the roll.
  • 3. The Re-admission charge is Rs. 200/-
